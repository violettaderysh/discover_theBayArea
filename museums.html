The Best Hiking Trails in the Bay Area

Must-visit museums and galleries with everything from ancient artifacts to modern art installations

Computer History Museum

Mountain View, CA

Dedicated to preserving the history of computing and its societal impact.

  • Explore the evolution of computers, software, and the internet.
  • See vintage hardware like early PCs and supercomputers.
  • Participate in hands-on programming workshops.
VISIT

Exploratorium

Pier 15, San Francisco, CA

A hands-on museum blending science, art, and human perception.

  • Discover interactive exhibits on light, sound, and physics.
  • Enjoy outdoor displays along San Francisco Bay.
  • Experience the iconic Tactile Dome.
VISIT

San Francisco Museum of Modern Art

San Francisco, CA

A world-renowned museum dedicated to modern and contemporary art.

  • Over 33,000 works of art, including photography, paintings, and design.
  • Stunning architecture and rooftop garden.
  • Frequent new exhibitions featuring international artists.
VISIT

Hiller Aviation Museum

San Carlos, CA

Focuses on the history of aviation, especially in California.

  • Historical aircraft displays, including prototypes and drones.
  • Flight simulators and interactive exhibits.
  • Kid-friendly activities and educational programs.
VISIT

Legion of Honor

San Francisco, CA

A fine arts museum with European art, ancient artifacts, and a stunning location.

  • Extensive collection of paintings, sculptures, and decorative arts.
  • Rodin’s The Thinker prominently displayed.
  • Spectacular views of the Golden Gate Bridge.
VISIT

Cantor Arts Center

Palo Alto, CA

Stanford University’s art museum featuring a diverse collection of global art.

  • A large collection of Rodin sculptures, including The Gates of Hell.
  • Free admission for all visitors.
  • Special exhibitions on contemporary and historical art.
VISIT

California Academy of Sciences

Golden Gate Park, San Francisco, CA

A unique museum combining an aquarium, planetarium, and natural history exhibits.

  • A four-story rainforest dome.
  • The Steinhart Aquarium, featuring diverse marine life.
  • A living roof covered in native plants.
VISIT

USS Hornet Museum

Alameda, CA

A historic aircraft carrier turned museum, highlighting naval history and space exploration.

  • Exhibits on World War II, Apollo moon missions, and Navy operations.
  • Access to aircraft on the flight deck.
  • Guided tours through the ship’s interior.
VISIT

Oakland Museum of California

Oakland, CA

A museum celebrating California’s art, history, and natural sciences.

  • Interactive exhibits exploring California’s diverse cultures and ecology.
  • A peaceful garden with local plants.
  • Popular Friday Nights @ OMCA events with food and music.
VISIT
